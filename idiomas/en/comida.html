EASY RECIPES TO MAKE AT CHRISTMAS

Ingredients for ROSCÓN DE REYES:

160 ml warm milk
90 g (for the roscón)
12 g fresh yeast or 4 g dry baker’s yeast
90 g sugar
400 g all-purpose wheat flour
15 ml orange blossom water
Zest of 1 orange (no white part)
3 medium egg yolks (about 60 g each including shell)
20 ml (1 + 1/2 tbsp) extra virgin olive oil
6 g salt
50 g softened butter

INGREDIENTS FOR TURKEY:

1 turkey
1 cup fresh orange juice
1 cup white wine
1 tbsp chicken stock powder
1 tsp ground cumin
2 bay leaves
5 black peppercorns
1 tbsp lemon juice
1/2 onion
1 garlic clove
2 tbsp coarse salt

Ingredients for ROAST LAMB:

1 lamb shoulder or leg
10 garlic cloves
1 tbsp oregano
1 tbsp parsley
1 glass white wine
A splash of brandy
Olive oil
Salt
Pepper

INGREDIENTS FOR PERNIL:

Pork pernil (pork shoulder/leg)
Onion (2)
Garlic (2 cloves)
Peppercorns
Ginger
Port wine
Vegetable stock
Potato
Parsley
Garlic (1 clove)

Ingredients for BAKED SEA BREAM:

1 sea bream (size as you like)
5 garlic cloves
1 cayenne chili (amount to taste)
Salt
Ground black pepper
50 ml white wine vinegar
120 ml extra virgin olive oil

INGREDIENTS FOR PANETTONE:

2 large eggs
1 large egg yolk
120 g sugar (1/2 cup)
10 g dry yeast (1 tbsp) or 30 g fresh yeast
1 tbsp vanilla extract
About 20 g honey (1 tbsp)
180 ml milk (2/3 cup)
80 g unsalted butter (about 3 tbsp heaping)
About 500 g all-purpose flour (about 3 + 1/2 cups)
About 200 g chocolate chips
Small pieces of unsalted butter to place on top of the panettones
