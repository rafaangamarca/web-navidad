CHRISTMAS IN NORWAY

Christmas in Norway


Christmas in Norway is mainly celebrated on December 24 and 25. It is a traditional, calm, and family-centered holiday, strongly shaped by winter, snow, and old customs.



MAIN NORWEGIAN TRADITIONS

  • Christmas Eve (Julaften): the most important day, with a family dinner and gifts.
  • Christmas tree: it is decorated and people gather around it singing carols.
  • Church visits: a religious tradition for many families.
  • Folk beliefs: in the past, people left food for spirits or animals.
  • Simple decorations: warm lights and natural ornaments.

CHRISTMAS CELEBRATION

Norwegian Christmas is cozy and calm. Families gather at home, enjoy a hearty dinner, and spend time together. The atmosphere is intimate and warm, perfect for winter, with candles, carols, and traditions that focus on comfort and unity.
