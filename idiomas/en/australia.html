CHRISTMAS IN AUSTRALIA

Christmas in Australia


Christmas in Australia is celebrated on December 25, but it happens during summer. It is a family holiday with a relaxed vibe, often outdoors, at the beach, or in gardens.



MAIN AUSTRALIAN TRADITIONS

  • Christmas barbecues: outdoor meals with family and friends.
  • Christmas at the beach: many people spend the day on the coast.
  • Outdoor carols: concerts and popular community singing.
  • Summer Santa Claus: shown in light clothing or even on a surfboard.
  • Decorations: trees and lights adapted to the summer weather.


TRADITIONAL AUSTRALIAN CHRISTMAS FOOD

  • Grilled meats: seafood, lamb, or sausages.
  • Fresh seafood: prawns and shrimp are very popular.
  • Salads and cold dishes: because of the heat.
  • Pavlova: a typical dessert with meringue, cream, and fruit.
  • Cold drinks: soft drinks and juices.


CHRISTMAS CELEBRATION

Australian Christmas is informal and cheerful. Families get together to eat outdoors, enjoy the sun, and spend time together. The atmosphere is festive but calm, very different from the winter Christmas celebrated in other countries.
