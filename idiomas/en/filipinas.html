CHRISTMAS IN THE PHILIPPINES

Christmas in the Philippines


Christmas in the Philippines is one of the longest in the world, starting in September and ending in January. It is a very religious, family-centered, and joyful celebration, with lots of music and community gatherings.



MAIN FILIPINO TRADITIONS

  • Simbang Gabi: a series of early-morning masses before Christmas.
  • Nochebuena: a family dinner after the mass on December 24.
  • Parol: star-shaped lanterns that decorate homes and streets.
  • Christmas carols: very popular singing traditions.
  • Gift-giving: especially for children.

CHRISTMAS CELEBRATION

Christmas in the Philippines is very community- and family-oriented. People attend mass, decorate with parol, and share big meals after Nochebuena. The atmosphere is joyful, full of music, faith, and togetherness, with a strong sense of unity.
